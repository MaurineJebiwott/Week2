History

Nestled in the heart of a historic district, Ember & Thyme began as a humble family-run eatery in 1985. Originally a small bakery known for its wood-fired bread and herbal-infused dishes, it quickly became a local favorite. Over the years, the restaurant expanded its menu, blending traditional flavors with modern culinary techniques. The name Ember & Thyme reflects its roots—honoring the warmth of the fire that has cooked its meals for decades and the fragrant herbs that define its signature dishes. Today, it stands as a beloved dining spot, preserving its rustic charm while offering a refined farm-to-table experience.

Our Mission

At Ember & Thyme, our mission is to create unforgettable dining experiences by blending tradition with innovation. We are committed to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to craft dishes that celebrate flavor, sustainability, and community. Through warm hospitality and a passion for culinary artistry, we strive to bring people together, one meal at a time.
